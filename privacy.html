WeTow - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

POPIA COMPLIANT - SOUTH AFRICA

WeTow is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information

Account Information:

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Vehicle details (make, model, registration)
  • Payment information (encrypted)

Driver Information:

  • Driver's license, vehicle registration
  • Insurance details, bank account details
  • Background check results

1.2 Location Information

We collect location data to match you with nearby Drivers, calculate pricing, and provide navigation assistance. Location tracking occurs only when you request a service or while service is being provided.

1.3 Device Information

We automatically collect device type, operating system, unique identifiers, IP address, and app usage data.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Service Delivery: Match Users with Drivers, calculate pricing, process payments
  • Safety & Security: Verify Driver credentials, investigate incidents, respond to panic alerts
  • Improvements: Analyze usage patterns, improve service quality
  • Communications: Send service updates, receipts, important notices

3. INFORMATION SHARING

3.1 With Drivers

When you request service, we share your name, phone number, pickup location, and vehicle information with the assigned Driver.

3.2 With Service Providers

We share information with trusted partners:

  • Payment processors (for transactions)
  • Cloud hosting providers (Firebase/Google Cloud)
  • Background check providers (for Driver verification)

3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information if required by court orders, law enforcement requests, or to protect rights and safety.

4. DATA SECURITY

We protect your information using:

  • Encryption (data in transit and at rest)
  • Secure servers with firewalls
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

5. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER POPIA

Under South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your information
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@wetow.co.za

6. DATA RETENTION

  • Active accounts: Duration of account existence
  • Closed accounts: 7 years for legal/tax purposes
  • Transaction records: 7 years
  • Location data: 90 days

7. MARKETING COMMUNICATIONS

We may send promotional emails, SMS, or push notifications. You can opt-out anytime by:

  • Clicking "unsubscribe" in emails
  • Adjusting notification settings in the App
  • Contacting support@wetow.co.za

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

WeTow is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your information may be stored on servers outside South Africa (Google Cloud/Firebase in United States). We ensure adequate protection through POPIA-compliant data transfer agreements.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes through in-app notifications or email.
